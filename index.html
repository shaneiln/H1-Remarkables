What is H1?

What is H1 and why do you need it?

H1 is the New Zealand Building Code clause governing energy efficiency in residential buildings. It requires new homes to meet minimum thermal performance standards across the building envelope: walls, roof, floor, windows, and doors. The Calculation Method under Acceptable Solution H1/AS1 is now the primary compliance pathway, following the removal of the Schedule Method in November 2025. An H1 compliance report is required for any residential building consent application — without it, your consent won't be processed.
